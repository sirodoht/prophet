128k.blog — long-form nostr

We should have long-form nostr, too, right?

This is an one-day attempt at a blogging client with nostr. Of course, you can't edit posts. Who needs edit? Nothing can be unseen, anyway xD

What you can do is post something to your existing nostr account. You can choose your relays, too!

Open source forever, on github.com/sirodoht/prophet and on git.sr.ht/~sirodoht/prophet (for the hipsters—myself included).

Don't use it for evil!
